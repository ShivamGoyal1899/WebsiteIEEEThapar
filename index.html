About Us

You Can Build The Tech

At IEEE, we are dedicated to serving the purpose of helping its members to enrich their technical knowledge and expertise. The main focus of this association is to conduct social and technical activities for students and create awareness and encourages them to utilize the benefits of IEEE membership, including scholarships, competitions, and conference grants.

Our Mission

IEEE Student Branch TIET seeks to cultivate the culture of research and development, to encourage the environment of experimentation and novelty for a greater cause, to organize workshops, hands-on sessions etc. in order to acquaint the students with recent trends and development in technology.
Dr. Ashima Singh | Assistant Professor, Thapar Institute
From the desk of Student Chair

Our Vision

IEEE Student Branch TIET seeks to contribute towards the technological development worldwide and intriguing the minds of students for research and innovation for the betterment of humanity leading to global prosperity and to promote engineering process of developing, sharing and applying knowledge for the better future.
